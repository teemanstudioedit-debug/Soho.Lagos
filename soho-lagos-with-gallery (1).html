Our Story

A New Standard
in Lagos Dining

SOHŌ Lagos occupies a singular space in Lekki's dining scene — equal parts restaurant, lounge, and destination. From the warm wooden tones and wine wall to the minimalist lighting and indoor greenery, every detail is considered. It is a space for conversation, celebration, and memorable evenings.

  • Contemporary upscale cuisine with international and American-inspired influences
  • Chic, minimalist interiors designed for intimacy and occasion
  • An extensive wine and cocktail programme curated for the discerning Lagos guest
  • Ideal for date nights, corporate dinners, birthdays, and private celebrations
  • ₦800 packaging fee for takeaway orders

The SOHŌ Experience

More than a meal —
a moment

01
The Ambience

Warm wooden tones, clean beige palette, minimalist lighting and curated indoor plants create a contemporary luxury setting that is Instagram-ready yet never try-hard. A space built for genuine connection.

02
The Cuisine

An international menu with bold American and contemporary influences. Every plate is crafted for both flavour and presentation — from the crispy tiger prawns to the showstopping wagyu ribeye.

03
The Bar

An open wine wall, signature cocktails, and a premium spirits list. Our bar team pairs knowledgeable recommendations with a welcoming approach — whether your evening begins or ends here.

04
The Service

Attentive without intrusion. Our team is trained to anticipate and deliver — ensuring that from arrival to farewell, your experience at SOHŌ is one you will return to.

05
Private Events

Birthdays, anniversaries, corporate gatherings — SOHŌ hosts with distinction. Tailored packages available on request. Speak to our events team for a bespoke proposal.

06
Nightlife

As the evening deepens, SOHŌ transforms. A fashionable crowd, carefully curated music, and a lounge atmosphere that keeps Lagos's most discerning guests coming back.
